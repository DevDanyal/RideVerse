Every NPC should have realistic behavior, schedules, emotions, conversations, and reactions.

NPCs should not simply stand still—they should live their daily lives.

# Multiplayer

Every player sees synchronized NPC positions.

Important NPC actions are synchronized by the server.

Mission NPCs remain consistent for all players.

---

# Performance

Only nearby NPCs remain active.

Distant NPCs use simplified AI.

Invisible NPCs are unloaded when appropriate.

---

# Security

NPC behavior is controlled by the server.

Clients cannot modify NPC states.

Mission NPCs are server-authoritative.

# AI Rules

Every NPC has a unique UUID.

NPC decisions are validated by the backend.

Never duplicate NPC IDs.

NPC schedules must remain synchronized.

Always optimize NPC performance for mobile devices.
