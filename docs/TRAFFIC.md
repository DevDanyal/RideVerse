Version: 1.0.0

# Vision

The traffic system makes RideVerse feel like a real living city.

Vehicles should follow traffic rules, react to players, obey traffic lights, avoid accidents, and dynamically adapt to road conditions.

Traffic must be intelligent, optimized, and synchronized for multiplayer.
