Version: 1.0.0

# Vision

The bike system is one of the most important parts of RideVerse.

Every bike should feel realistic in appearance, sound, physics, handling, and customization.

The player should feel like they are riding a real motorcycle.

# AI Rules

Every bike has a unique ID.

All bike modifications are validated by the backend.

The client cannot modify bike statistics directly.

Physics calculations must be server-validated where required.

Never duplicate bike data.
