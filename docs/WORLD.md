# RideVerse World Design

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Planning

---

# Vision

RideVerse is a realistic open-world online multiplayer game where players can freely explore, work, race, complete missions, own vehicles, buy properties, and interact with thousands of other players.

The world should feel alive 24 hours a day.

---

# World Goals

The world must be:

- Realistic
- Large
- Expandable
- Multiplayer Friendly
- Optimized for Mobile
- Dynamic
- Interactive
- Fun to Explore

---

# World Structure

The world is divided into regions.

Example:

Region 1 - Starter City

Region 2 - Downtown

Region 3 - Residential Area

Region 4 - Industrial Area

Region 5 - Mountain Roads

Region 6 - Highway

Region 7 - Desert

Region 8 - Airport

Region 9 - Harbor
