# Vision

Cars in RideVerse should feel as realistic as possible.

Every car should have unique sounds, handling, acceleration, braking, suspension, and customization.

Players should experience realistic driving physics on mobile devices.

# AI Rules

Every vehicle has a unique UUID.

Vehicle ownership is validated by the backend.

Clients cannot modify vehicle statistics.

Performance calculations must be server-authoritative.

Never duplicate vehicle records.
