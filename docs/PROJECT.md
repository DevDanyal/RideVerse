# Purpose

RideVerse is a next-generation online open-world life simulation game where players can freely explore a living world, ride motorcycles, drive cars, complete missions, earn money, customize vehicles, purchase houses and businesses, interact with other players online, and build their own virtual life.

The goal is to create one of the most realistic and enjoyable mobile multiplayer games possible.

---

# Vision

RideVerse is not only a motorcycle game.

RideVerse is a complete life simulation.

Players should feel like they are living inside another world.

Everything should feel realistic.

Every decision should matter.

Every player should have their own story.
