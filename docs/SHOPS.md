# Vision

The shop system allows players to purchase, sell, upgrade, repair and customize everything they own.

Every shop should feel realistic and have its own purpose.

All purchases are validated by the backend.

# AI Rules

Every purchase is permanent unless refunded.

Every sale is logged.

All shop prices are controlled by the backend.

Clients cannot modify prices.

Never allow duplicated purchases.

Never allow negative balances.
